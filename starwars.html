Star Wars Jedi: Fallen Order is an action-adventure game that follows Cal Kestis, a young Jedi Padawan on the run after the Jedi Purge. Set between Episodes III and IV, the game combines lightsaber combat, Force abilities, and exploration across various Star Wars planets as Cal fights to survive, rebuild the Jedi Order, and confront his past while being hunted by the Empire's deadly Inquisitors.
