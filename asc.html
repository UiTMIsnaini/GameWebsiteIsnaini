Assassin’s Creed Unity is an action-adventure game set during the French Revolution, following Arno Dorian, a young Assassin seeking redemption and justice. The game features parkour-based exploration through 18th-century Paris, stealth missions, and dynamic melee combat, while weaving a story of political turmoil, personal revenge, and the fight between Assassins and Templars.
